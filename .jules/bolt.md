## 2024-07-25 - Unrelated Bugs Can Block Performance Work
**Learning:** I spent a significant amount of time trying to fix a rendering bug in `gravity.js` that was unrelated to my initial performance optimization. This prevented me from verifying my change and ultimately forced me to reset my work.
**Action:** In the future, if I encounter a complex, pre-existing bug that blocks my work, I will immediately revert my changes and find a different, verifiable performance optimization. It's better to deliver a smaller, clean win than to get bogged down in unrelated fixes.
